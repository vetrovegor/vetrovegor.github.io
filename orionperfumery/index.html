About orion perfumery About us

ORION PERFUMERY was founded in 2022 to bring Beauty ecommerce and traditional retailers across the world together with the biggest beauty brands to market, and distribute perfumes, skincare and cosmetics for men and women.

Beauty

The founder is George Pavlov with more than 23 years of experience in the Luxury Beauty industry. The emergence of niche brands and fashion companies, focused on their online presence and social media, has radically changed a fragrance market previously dominated by mass selective brands. The Millennium and Gen Z generations identify with brands through the internet and social media thus enabling independent ecommerce retail beauty companies to compete with larger groups. This was not possible previously via traditional media.

ORION PERFUMERY multi-brand strategy is to bring the biggest ecommerce beaty pure players directly with brands which are focused on high creativity in fashion, have a strong luxury image and are interested in developing a dynamic presence and sales online. The founder started his activities in this sector by distributing perfume and cosmetic brands in to one of the biggest beauty retailers in the world. This experience gave the founder an invaluable insight into the emergence and growth of the beauty market and brand building via a new a growing ecommerce and social media environment.
